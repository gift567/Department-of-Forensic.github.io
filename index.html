About Us

Ministry of Home Affairs & Internal Security Office of the State Forensic Pathologist Internal Security Office of the State Forensic Pathologist.

The Office of State Forensic Pathologist (OSFP) is an autonomous death investigation institution domiciled in the Ministry of Home Affairs and Internal Security and accountable to the Minister of Home Affairs and Internal Security per the National Forensic Act No.2, 2020 provisions. Forensic pathology services are aimed at contributing to the administration of Justice, crime prevention, and public safety by being impartial, professional, and accurate in medicolegal death investigations and certification.
