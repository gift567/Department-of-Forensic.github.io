Our Mission

To provide timely, professional, and scientifically accurate forensic pathology services that support the justice system and promote public health and safety in Zambia.

Our Vision

To be a leading forensic institution in Africa, recognized for excellence in medico-legal investigations and trusted by all sectors of society.

Our Core Values

Integrity, impartiality, scientific rigor, confidentiality, professionalism, and respect for human dignity in every examination and report we deliver.
